About Central University of Haryana

Central University of Haryana (CUH) is a prestigious institution established in 2009 by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. Accredited with an 'A' Grade by NAAC and recognized in the NIRF rankings, CUH is committed to providing high-quality education and fostering a dynamic learning environment.

Located on a sprawling 484-acre campus in Mahendergarh, Haryana, CUH offers a diverse range of academic programs. With a focus on research and innovation, the university strives to empower students and contribute to the advancement of knowledge.
